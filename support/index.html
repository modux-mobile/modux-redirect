Frequently Asked Questions

How do I connect Strava?

Open MODUX and go to the Profile tab. Tap "Connect" next to the Strava row. You'll be redirected to Strava's website to authorize MODUX. Once authorized, you'll be sent back to the app and your running activities will sync automatically. You can tap "Sync Strava activities" at any time to pull in the latest runs.

How do I connect Apple Health?

Open MODUX and go to the Profile tab. Tap "Connect" next to the Apple Health row. iOS will show a permission sheet asking which data MODUX can read. Enable the categories you'd like to share (workouts, heart rate, distance, energy burned) and tap "Allow." Once connected, tap "Sync Apple Health" to import your recent runs.

How do I delete my account and data?

The fastest way is right in the app: open the Profile tab and tap "Delete Account" — deletion is immediate and permanent. You can also email tave2000@icloud.com with the subject line "MODUX Data Deletion Request" and we'll delete all your data within 14 days.

Why don't my runs show up after syncing?

A few things to check:

  • Make sure you have an internet connection — syncing requires network access to reach our servers.
  • For Strava: verify the app shows "Connected" on the Profile tab. If it says "Not connected," tap Connect to re-authorize.
  • For Apple Health: confirm you've granted MODUX read access in Settings > Privacy & Security > Health > MODUX.
  • If a run was already imported from another source (e.g., it appears in both Strava and Apple Health), MODUX automatically deduplicates it so it only shows once.

If none of the above helps, email us and we'll investigate.
